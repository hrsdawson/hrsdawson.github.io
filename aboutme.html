About me

I'm a physical oceanographer with an interest in the large-scale circulation and dynamics of polar regions. I currently work as a postdoc at the Institute for Marine and Antarctic Studies in nipaluna/Hobart, where I use coupled climate models and ocean-sea ice models to investigate the drivers of Antarctic sea ice variability.

When not working, I might be found hiking, climbing, crafting (many things, but especially sewing), swimming in the ocean (though much less so in lutruwita/Tasmania), or eating (preferrably Pastel de nata from Tuga pastries).
